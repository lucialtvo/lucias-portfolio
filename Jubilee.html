Graphic and Branding Design

Jubilee

Figma, Adobe Illustrator

by Lucia Vo

OVERVIEW

In just four days, I designed the logo and branding for Youth Expo 2025: Jubilee, an annual Catholic youth event that gathers over 50 participants and volunteers for a day of activities, community, and reflection. The theme, “Jubilee”, was inspired by the wider "Pilgrims of Hope" initiative and inspired by the bible verse, “Be strong and take heart, all you who hope in the Lord” (Psalm 31:24). My task was to translate this theme into a visual identity that felt both celebratory and welcoming, all while juggling a strict deadline.

My design process for tackling the Jubilee brand began by asking: What would it look like if hope and celebration were expressed through design?

Meeting with Cecilia, the event coodinator, and my rough sketches of initial logo ideas.

The process started with meeting with Cecilia, the ministry and event coordinator, to unpack the meaning of Jubilee and how it could shape the event’s look and feel. Since this was a style I hadn’t really tried before, I still approached it like any of my other design processes: sketching out rough ideas in my notebook, experimenting with different visual flavours and interpretations, then piling some potential inspiration into a moodboard. Those early explorations laid the first bricks for the event’s identity.

My initial Pinterest moodboard and Figma brainstorming document for the Youth Expo logo.

WHERE DID THE TIME GO?

Pushing to two days left, I moved into digital iterations. Using Figma and Illustrator, I played with vibrant shapes, colors, and typography, producing multiple logo interpretations of “Jubilee.” Cecilia requested for variations in color while maintaining the doodle theme, which pushed me to explore outside my comfort zone and try new approaches to this style. With each iteration, I kept refining the design toward something that balanced joy, faith, and approachability for a youth audience.

Many iterations of the Jubilee logo in Adobe Illustrator.

LET'S CELEBRATE!

The "Jubilee" Youth Expo 2025 logo branding uses bold, rounded typography and hand-drawn playful doodle accents, such as fireworks, stars, and sparks, to capture the energy of a youth celebration. A flame detail over the “i” symbolizes hope, while bright accent colors reference the "Pilgrims of Hope" theme to balance the grounded maroon palette. Together, the logo and supporting graphics create a joyful, welcoming identity that unites the event’s theme with a festival-like spirit.

The final logo, as well as its matching bible verse.

TAKEAWAYS

Brick by brick, I realized that big challenges aren’t as intimidating once you break them into smaller goals. Even through a tight deadline, designing the Jubilee branding taught me how to work step by step and trust that the pieces would come together in the end.

Another thing I learned is that you don’t always realize how impactful design can be until you see it out in the world. Watching the Jubilee branding come to life on shirts, stickers, and in the hands of participants felt like my own celebration!

Design to reality: me with the finished shirts and participants bringing them to life!
